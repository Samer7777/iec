The Islamic Educational College (IEC) is located in both Jabal Amman and Al-Jubaiha and is under the administration of the Islamic Cultural Society (ICS). The Islamic Cultural Society thrives to provide full support to IEC schools by meeting all their needs.

1947

Inaugurated by His Majesty the late King Abdullah I bin Al Hussein.

Founded by the Islamic cultural Society, The Islamic Educational College- Amman opened  its doors in 1947 for all the citizens of Jordan (non Jordanians also) with only 155 students. It was opened by His Majesty peace be upon him King Abdullah the founder.We are honerd to have His Majesty King Hussien -peace be upon him- as one of our students at that time.

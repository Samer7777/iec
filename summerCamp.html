Welcome to IEC Summer Camp 2024!

The school offers a summer camp that aims at enriching the students’ time with useful activities throughout summer. The camp includes different field trips activities in addition to an actual sleep over

scouting camp at the school premises. 

We are proud that our students and volunteers are trained to become our future coaches. 

With its lush green fields, professional staff, and spacious facilities equipped with all the necessary amenities, we guarantee an enjoyable and comfortable experience for all campers.

The fun never stops at our Summer Camp and we are all awaiting to welcome your loved little ones aged 3-16 partaking in a wide range of activities to enhance their technical, physical, and mental development.

In today’s world, the benefits of building character skills and traits such as gratitude, kindness, creativity, curiosity, perseverance, resiliency, empathy, courage, and teamwork could not be more important.
